Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Why Play?

Match Match is a very simple and fun game that almost anyone could pick up! Since the rules are so simple you can play with multiple players very easily. You can also play by yourself! Not only is this a fun and competitive game, it also comes with other benefits. Match Match has been proven to improve concentration, train visual memory, increase short term memory, increase attention to detail and even improve vocabulary.
